Privacy Policy for WebConvert+

Last Updated: December 4, 2025

WebConvert+ ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome Extension handles your data.

1. Data Collection and Usage
WebConvert+ is designed as a client-side extension. It scans the text of web pages you visit to identify and convert currencies, units, and dates. This processing happens entirely within your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit your browsing history or personal data to our servers.

2. External Services
The extension uses the following third-party service solely for fetching currency exchange rates:
- Frankfurter API (https://api.frankfurter.app): We fetch daily exchange rates from this public API. No personal data is sent with these requests.

3. Privacy Mode
We offer a "Privacy Mode" setting. When enabled, the extension will block all external network requests, including exchange rate updates. In this mode, the extension relies on cached or default exchange rates.
